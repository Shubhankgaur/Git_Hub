Delicious Pancake Recipe

This is a simple and quick recipe to make fluffy pancakes.

Total Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Preparation

  1. Whisk together dry ingredients.

    2 minutes

  2. Mix wet ingredients in another bowl.

    3 minutes

  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.

    1 minute

  4. Heat a skillet and pour batter to form pancakes.

    10 minutes

  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.

    4 minutes
